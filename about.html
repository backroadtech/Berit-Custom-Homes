The Berit Team

Roel Moreno

Founder & CEO

Mr. Moreno serves in all areas of the business which includes, Sales, Project Management, Marketing, and Project Development at Berit. Clients can expect to get transparency and honesty regarding the overall process of starting and building a home. Mr. Moreno has a great deal of passion for helping the community and the people in it when it comes to fulfilling their dreams of building a home. Mr. Moreno enjoys striving towards helping families and individuals live out the American dream of becoming homeowners. This is because he believes that owning home is a part of establishing and building memories that lead to a greater delight in life.

Ruben Fabela

Sales Manager

Mr. Fabela has a passion for helping clients not only build their future home but have them enjoy and embrace the experience of what it means to become a homeowner. Mr. Fabela takes pride in ensuring that the client has clear direction and confidence in the building process. His clients can expect reliability in knowing that he has their best interest in mind, along with his availability to be there 24/7 for his clients’ questions and concerns about their home. Mr. Fabela knows the importance and value it has to be given the privilege to building someone’s home and that is why one can expect dedication and diligence when working with Mr. Fabela.

Tony Garcia

Sales Associate

Mr. Garcia has had a long career in Hospitality Service in where he has been able to develop and build a standard of what it means to display care in the service that is being offered to the client. He takes joy in knowing that the client knows exactly where they stand in when building their home. Mr. Garcia ensures that every one of his client’s needs are met. He understands that purchasing a home can be a big step, and so that is why he works towards ensuring that his clients feel 100% confident in the purchase of their home. Mr. Garcia has also firsthand experience with the building process and helps shed light to his clients when it comes to making decisions on their home.
